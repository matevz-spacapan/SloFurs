SloFurs Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Društvo SloFurs uses the personal data we collect about you when you visit our website.

Key information from the privacy policy

  • Why do we need your data? We need your data in order to contact you in case of force majeure events and to report any disputes or damages to the relevant authorities.
  • How long do we retain your data? We retain the data on our website until you delete it. You will be provided with the option to delete your data within 10 days after the completion of the last event you registered for.
  • Who has access to your data? Only the website administrators and event organizers have access to your data.

The entity responsible for this website is Društvo SloFurs (SloFurs association), located at Gregorčičeva ulica 33, 5000 Nova Gorica, Slovenia.

Email address: info@slofurs.org

Društvo SloFurs collects the following data:
  • Personal identification information (such as name, email address, address, phone number, etc.),
  • your current and past event registrations, and
  • personal preferences for events (such as food and beverage preferences, preferences for social activities, etc.).
You provide us with personal data directly. We collect and manage data about you when you:
  • create an account on our website,
  • complete your user profile with personal information,
  • register for an event,
  • voluntarily complete a survey about an event or provide feedback through online chat platforms (e.g., Discord) or via email, and
  • use or browse our website using browser cookies.
Društvo SloFurs collects your data in order to:
  • process and manage your account,
  • create a statement for you to sign at the event if required,
  • process online payments,
  • email you with upcoming events held by us,
  • email you with important updates for events you registered for.

We require this information to contact you in case of emergencies and to report any disputes or damages to the appropriate authorities.

A small portion of your data is shared with the SendGrid service for the purpose of sending email communications, but the service does not retain this data.

We do not share any of your data with third parties or external platforms. Only the event organizers and website administrators have access to the data you provide.

When we process event registrations and receive online payments, we may also receive associated data from the payment processor (e.g. Stripe), such as your name, email address, and billing address stored by the payment processor. However, Društvo SloFurs does not receive or process any payment or credit card data.

Društvo SloFurs securely stores your data at our server provider.

Društvo SloFurs will retain your personal data from the moment you provide it to us until you choose to delete it.

You can delete your personal data on the user pages, but this option will not be available to you if you have registered for upcoming events or if the last attended event has concluded within the past 10 days.

The statement that you are required to sign before participating at an event will be retained by the organizer for a period of at least 10 days after the event has ended, and it may be retained indefinitely if you have chosen to sign a permanent statement. The organizer will destroy the statement after this period or in the case of a permanent statement upon your withdrawal from it.

Društvo SloFurs would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:
  • The right to access – You have the right to request SloFurs to give you copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that SloFurs correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request SloFurs to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request that SloFurs erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that SloFurs restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to SloFurs' processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that SloFurs transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: info@slofurs.org.

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org

How do we use cookies?

Društvo SloFurs uses cookies only to keep you signed in to your account.

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Session cookies – SloFurs uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember who you are for the active user session. When you close your browser, these session cookies are deleted.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, some of our website features may not function as a result.

The SloFurs website may contain links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

IX. Changes to our privacy policy

Društvo SloFurs regularly checks and updates its privacy policy. The latest version is available on this page. The policy was last updated on May 26, 2023. In the latest revision of the policy, we have corrected various grammatical errors, provided additional explanations on how and why we use your data, and described the methods of storing the event statement.

X. How to contact us

If you have any questions about SloFurs' privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Email us at: info@slofurs.org
